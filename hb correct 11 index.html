1.) Who painted this?

Correct

Answer: Hyman Bloom

Hyman Bloom is a Boston Expressionist who was born in Latvia and raised in an Orthodox Jewish Family. His family immigrated here in 1920. Jackson Pollock and Willem De Koonig both consider him to the first Abstract Expressionist painter in America. He is a very expressive painter.

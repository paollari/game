1.) Who painted this?

Answer: Romare Bearden

As Jacob Lawrence was to the Great Migration, Romare Bearden was to the Harlem Renaissance. He portrayed not only every day life in but the movement of the people as well. He is most well known for his collage work which highlights the pace of Harlem at that time.
